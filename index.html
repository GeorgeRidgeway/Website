Abstract: Hello! For those of you who don't know me, my name is George Ridgeway and this is my website. I started working on this site as I was completing my Master's degree as a way to showcase the work I have done as well as the skills I have learned along the way. But first, a bit about my background. I attended Stetson University where I graduated cum laude with a Bachelor of Science degree in physics. While at Stetson, I applied for and received funding to undertake a biophysics summer research project that focused on cell growth. Additionally, I also completed a senior research project focusing on high-resolution planetary imaging. After graduating, I worked for a few months as a Sample Receiving Technician and a Quality Control Analyst. While working, I was also submitting applications for graduate school.
