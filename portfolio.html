Dn4000 Calculator
  • Dn4000 is the spectral ratio of the red side (4000Å to 4100Å) to the blue side (3850Å to 3950Å) of a galaxy spectrum.
  • By measuring the strength of the 4000Å break, we are able to trace the stellar population age of a galaxy.
  • A more in-depth discussion about Dn4000 can be found in Balogh et al. 1999, Kauffmann et al. 2003, and Mendel et al. 2013.
  • Analyzed results of KDE compared to true properties of galaxies.
